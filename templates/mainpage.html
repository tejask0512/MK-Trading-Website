Our Story

About MK Trading Academy

Welcome to MK Trading Academy

Your next door pitstop to the world of stock market, established for the very first time in India. MK Trading stands as the premier institute for stock market education in India, offering the most comprehensive online stock market course available.

Our mission is to empower the younger generation, providing them with the knowledge and tools to achieve financial independence and success. We are dedicated to those who are ambitious, eager to learn, and ready to take control of their financial future.

Trading is often perceived as a business that isn't suitable for everyone, but this is a misconception we aim to dispel. Our program focuses on teaching long-tested strategies that have consistently proven to be profitable. Through our comprehensive curriculum, we equip our students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the stock market.

Kunal Shivaji Biradar

CEO at MK Trading Academy

Over the years, Kunal developed a realistic, in-depth, and time-tested strategy, shaped by his own hands-on expertise in the industry. His broad and varied experience in the markets has given him a thorough understanding of how things work, allowing him to create plans that aren't just theoretical, but truly effective.

But he isn't just about crafting strategies—he's also passionate about teaching. He knows that the stock market can seem intimidating, so he's made it a point to break down complex ideas into simpler, more accessible concepts.

His courses are designed to be approachable for everyone, no matter how much—or how little—they know going in. Kunal's teaching style is straightforward and engaging, making it easier for aspiring traders to grasp the ins and outs of the stock market.

Over time, he has taught more than 400 students, helping them develop the skills they need to succeed as traders. His dedication to his students and their growth is clear in everything he does.
